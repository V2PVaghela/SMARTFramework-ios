  • – fire

  • – finishWith:

    A method to finish the call early, but successfully. Use abortWithError: to finish a call early and unsuccessfully. This method provokes the same actions as if it was fired and returned successfully. This method is NOT being called when the URL connection finishes.
  • – cancel

    Cancels the call; calling “abortWithError:nil” has the same effect
  • – abortWithError:

    This method can be called to abort a call and have it send the provided error with its callback
